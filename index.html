// Core Principles

Dalio's Economic Machine

Every decision is grounded in Ray Dalio's proven macro-economic framework.

▲ Growth Rising + Inflation Rising

Overweight commodities, TIPS, emerging market equities. The economy is heating up — ride the momentum while hedging inflation risk.

▲ Growth Rising + Inflation Falling

Overweight equities, corporate bonds. The goldilocks environment — lean into risk assets as conditions favor broad expansion.

▼ Growth Falling + Inflation Rising

Overweight gold, commodities, inflation-linked bonds. Stagflation territory — protect capital and hedge against purchasing power erosion.

▼ Growth Falling + Inflation Falling

Overweight nominal bonds, defensive equities. Deflationary risk — prioritize capital preservation and safe-haven positions.
